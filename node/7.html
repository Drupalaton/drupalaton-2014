Cheppers

Cheppers is a Hungary-based Drupal shop working in the heart of Budapest with an awesome team of 15 Drupal experts. For our clients who come from Europe and North America we provide custom Drupal solutions by developing their corporate websites, e-commerce sites and other web applications.

We love Drupal and we contribute modules, participate in the issue queues, post patches, edit documentation, and provide support on forums and on IRC channels. We are also regular attendees/speakers/sponsors/code sprinters at local and international Drupal events.

By sponsoring Drupalaton 2014 we would like to show our passion for the Drupal project and help the Hungarian and international communities to share and deepen their knowledge of Drupal 8.
